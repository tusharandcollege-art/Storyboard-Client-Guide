## Scene 7 — ⏱️ 14:35 | Chowk / Pandal Overlay

**Objective:** Historical festival pandal reconstruct karna modern chowk footage ke upar

- ✅ Fire tenders visible in background (period-authentic detail)
- ✅ Pandal towering over crowd
- ❌ No people standing on top of pandal structure
